Classic Interiors is a full service space planning, interior designing and contract firm. Classic Interiors is a midsized firm specializing in, Showrooms, Residential, Offices, Educational Institutes ,ATM’s and Modular kitchens with emphasis on renovation, adoptive reuse and corporate interiors. Classic Interiors is a partnership firm. Rajiv Chopra and Sameer Mehrotra are two partners who have done 2 years Diploma courses in interior designing.

We would consider it our privilege to offer professional consultancy vis a vis Interior designing and supervision. We would be highly obliged for an early reply so that we could arrange for a brief presentation of our works or provide more information and feedback. We look forward to the opportunity of participating in the growth process of your company of professional design colleagues.
